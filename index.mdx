**The Internet was built for navigation, not execution.** Every digital task whether swapping tokens, booking flights, or assembling a market report forces users to click through dashboards, tabs, and forms. AIVA flips that model. It is a **Composable Interface Protocol** that turns natural‑language intent into a live, purpose‑built UI that already knows how to act.

_Zero‑Click Computing_ → speak or type, AIVA handles the rest.
